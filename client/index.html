Our Design Goals

From the beginning, we were determined to build a fingerboard as beautiful and appealing as it is functional.

The result is the GripMonkeys fingerboard. It features subtle curves, clean lines and simple design. Made of hard beech wood it is sporting smooth holds and grippy sloppers.
